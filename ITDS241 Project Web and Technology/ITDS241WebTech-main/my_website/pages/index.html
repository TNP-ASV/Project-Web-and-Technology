Welcome to 73Car

Your Ultimate Destination for Quality Cars

73Gane Car

73GANE stands for Global Automotive Network Enterprise, and that's exactly what we are: a network of partners, suppliers, and customers who share a passion for cars and a vision for excellence. We started our business in 2015, with a small team of enthusiasts who wanted to bring the best cars from around the world to the Thai market. We began by importing luxury and sports cars from Europe and America, such as BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Porsche, Ferrari, and Lamborghini. We soon gained a reputation for delivering high-quality vehicles, with impeccable service and competitive prices.

Don't stop!

But we didn't stop there. We also saw an opportunity to expand our portfolio and cater to different segments of the market. We began to import more affordable and eco-friendly cars from Japan and Korea, such as Toyota, Honda, Nissan, Hyundai, and Kia. We also added some niche brands, such as Tesla, Mini, and Volvo. We wanted to offer our customers a wide range of choices, from the most practical to the most extravagant.

At Moment

Today, we have grown to become one of the largest and most trusted importers of cars from abroad to Thailand. We have a network of showrooms, dealerships, and service centers across the country. We have a loyal customer base, who appreciate our quality, reliability, and value. And we have a dedicated team of professionals, who work hard every day to make our customers happy.
